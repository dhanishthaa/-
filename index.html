The Atelier

Composed, not mixed.

Every Dhanishtha fragrance is blended in small ateliers between Grasse and Mumbai, then aged in cedar vats until the accord finds its balance. No shortcuts, no mass batches.

We work with a handful of perfumers who spend months on a single accord before it earns a number.
